# Speech Therapist
Speech Therapist: A speech therapist is a trained professional who helps individuals improve their communication skills. They work with students who have difficulties with speech, language, or swallowing, providing therapy to help them overcome these challenges. Speech therapists assess students' communication abilities and develop individualized plans to help them achieve their goals. They may work on articulation, fluency, voice, language development, and social communication skills. Speech therapists play a crucial role in supporting students with communication disorders and helping them succeed in their academic and social lives.
