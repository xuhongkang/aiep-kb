# Receipt of the Individualized Education Plan (IEP)
Receipt of the Individualized Education Plan (IEP): Receiving the Individualized Education Plan (IEP) means you get a special plan for your child at school. This plan is made just for your child to help them learn in the best way possible. It includes goals and ways to support your child's learning needs.

You can find more about this at: [https://drive.google.com/file/d/1au51uKAY-6t1Sabx47W3eCJkBBDn10ns/view#page=18](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1au51uKAY-6t1Sabx47W3eCJkBBDn10ns/view#page=18)
